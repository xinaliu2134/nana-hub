About

Built from the inside out.

I spent three years at SMOORE International Holdings — the world's largest manufacturer of vaping and heated tobacco products — as Head of Clinical Sciences. I led the scientific evaluation of dozens of products against FDA, EU, and global regulatory standards.

Before SMOORE, I completed my PhD in epidemiology at Erasmus MC (Rotterdam) and built a research career specializing in tobacco epidemiology and real-world evidence methodology. My academic foundation began with a medical degree from Peking University — and the academic work continues, with peer-reviewed publications across Nature Genetics, Frontiers in Pharmacology, PLoS One, and others.

I am a selected talent under the Guangdong Province Pearl River Talent Program (珠江计划) in recognition of contributions to tobacco regulatory science, and the founder of CSUR Research and Consultancy — an independent advisory practice serving NGP manufacturers, CROs, ingredient suppliers, and policy organizations.

Most of the tools on this site exist because a client needed them, or because I needed them and couldn't find an open version. I work with clients who want more than a consultant — they want someone who has sat in the room with the data, built the frameworks from scratch, and understands the difference between science that satisfies regulators and science that actually reduces harm.

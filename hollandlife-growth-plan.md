## Channel Strategy

### 1. XiaoHongShu (小红书) — Primary Growth Engine
**Why:** Largest platform for Chinese expats sharing overseas life experiences. High-intent audience actively searching for practical guides.

**Content Pillars:**
- "荷兰生活攻略" (NL Life Guides): BSN, DigiD, 30% ruling, housing
- "荷兰带娃日记" (NL Parenting Diary): schools, pediatricians, family activities
- "荷兰省钱秘籍" (NL Money-Saving Tips): insurance, taxes, shopping
- "荷兰文化冲击" (NL Culture Shock): funny/relatable stories that build trust

**Posting Schedule:** 3-4 posts/week, best times: 20:00-22:00 Beijing time (13:00-15:00 NL time)

**Format:**
- Cover image: clean, text-overlay style with consistent branding (blue + orange)
- Body: 300-500 characters, personal tone, bullet points, emoji
- Always end with CTA to HollandLife platform
- Hashtags: #荷兰 #荷兰生活 #荷兰华人 #海外生活 #欧洲移民

### 2. WeChat Ecosystem
**WeChat Official Account (公众号):**
- Publish 2 long-form articles/week (1,500-2,500 characters)
- Topics: deep-dive guides that XiaoHongShu posts can link to
- Build email-equivalent subscriber base

**WeChat Groups:**
- Join/create: "荷兰华人互助群", "荷兰新人群", city-specific groups (Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Den Haag, Eindhoven, Delft)
- Share helpful content (not spam), answer questions, build authority
- Partner with existing group admins

**Actions:**
